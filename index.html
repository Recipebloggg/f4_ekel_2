Creamy Game Day Guac

Description

Ingredients 6 people 3 avocados, ripe 1/2 red onion, diced fine 4 cloves garlic, minced 2 Roma tomatoes 1/3-1/2 cup cilantro 1 lime juiced 1 packet guac seasoning 1/2 cup sour cream Tortilla chips for serving

Cooking

1

Half the avocados. Remove pit and put in medium mixing bowl. Smash until they are the consistency you want.

2

Add diced tomatoes, onions, garlic, cilantro and juice of 1 lime. Mix together. Add seasoning and sour cream.

3

Cover and chill for at least 1 hour Serve with tortilla chips.
